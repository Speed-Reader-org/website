The Speed Reader macOS application processes all your reading content (files, URLs, clipboard text, screen captures) locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your reading content on our servers. The application does not include any analytics or tracking SDKs.

Chrome Extension

The Speed Reader Chrome extension processes webpage content locally in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store any content you read through the extension.
